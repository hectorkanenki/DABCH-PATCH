About Us

Keeping it blue.

The seas must reflect the clouds of the sky not reflect the ignorance of the human being. Creating awareness is essential for all mankind, to begin the change.

Mission

To transcend in the care and recovery of our oceans in a sustainable way. Reducing as never before any trace of human pollution generating a high level of environmental awareness in future generations, ensuring a legacy of well-being for marine species and humanity.

Vision

We see ourselves as a project that has achieved a deep cleaning not only in the oceans but in the values ​​of society as well which has sown a love for ocean life, hope for marine species and humanity.

Values

Our values ​​will keep us on track to meet our goals and generate the future that we visualize today. Social and environmental responsibility in the use of clean energy. Respect for marine life. Hope to always be able to meet our goals. Commitment of not resting until achieving it.
